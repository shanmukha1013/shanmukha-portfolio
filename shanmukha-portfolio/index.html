My Journey

Every experience, every challenge, and every project has shaped the engineer I'm becoming.

School

Building My Foundation

My school days taught me discipline, curiosity, and the habit of continuously learning new things. Even though I didn't know exactly what I wanted to become back then, those years helped me build the confidence to keep exploring and improving myself.

Intermediate

Choosing My Direction

During intermediate, I started thinking seriously about my future. Technology slowly caught my attention, and I realized I wanted to understand how software works and how ideas could become something people actually use.

2023

Exploring the Real World

My first year of engineering was all about exploring.

I participated in IEEE events, NSS activities, technical workshops, and college events whenever I had the opportunity. More importantly, I found a great group of friends and seniors who were always learning something new. Being around them changed the way I looked at learning and encouraged me to explore beyond the classroom.

2024

Discovering Innovation

This year completely changed my perspective.

I became part of my college Innovation Hub, where I learned that every successful product begins with a simple idea.

I learned a lot from seniors, mentors, and my teammates. We participated in hackathons, experimented with AI tools, built mini projects for college, and constantly shared ideas with each other. This was the year I stopped thinking only about writing code and started thinking about building products.

2025

Turning Ideas into Products

This was one of the most memorable years of my college life.

I became the captain of my college cricket team, which taught me leadership, teamwork, responsibility, and how to stay calm under pressure.

At the same time, I finally started working on my own ideas instead of only building college assignments. Everything I had learned from the Innovation Hub, hackathons, and my seniors slowly turned into real products. Around this time, I also developed a strong interest in Machine Learning and started building PunchStart, my first AI-powered product.

2026

Building My Future

I started this year with a much clearer goal than ever before.

Instead of simply learning new technologies, I began learning how real AI systems are designed and built.

I'm actively preparing for placements, participating in hackathons to learn rather than just compete, exploring Machine Learning, production AI systems, and software architecture, and continuously challenging myself with bigger projects.

Every month, I feel like I'm becoming a better engineer than I was before.

The Journey Continues...

I'm still in my final year, and I know this is only the beginning.

My next goal is to work with experienced engineers, build AI products that solve real-world problems, and continue turning ideas into products that people genuinely enjoy using.

"Still learning. Still building. Still turning ideas into products."
